About Pest Control Advisor Chatbot

Our Pest Control Advisor Chatbot is designed to provide comprehensive, eco-friendly pest management solutions. Here’s what it offers:

  • Pest Identification: Describe or upload pest pictures for identification (cockroaches, termites, etc.) and learn why they’re a problem.
  • Booking & Scheduling: Select dates/times, sync with technicians, and receive reminders.
  • Service Recommendations: Get tailored treatment plans, prevention tips, and advice for stings or bites.
  • Customer Support: Connect to live agents, access FAQs, and learn about our company.
  • Feedback & Emergency Services: Share reviews, get help for urgent infestations, and access chat history with login integration.

Our Services

We offer a range of pest control services through our chatbot:

  • Pest Identification: Upload pictures or describe pests for instant identification and info.
  • Booking Appointments: Schedule services with real-time technician availability.
  • Treatment Plans: Recommendations for one-time or recurring services, indoor/outdoor treatments.
  • Prevention Tips: Practical advice to keep pests away.
  • Emergency Help: Fast support for urgent infestations like bee swarms or rodents.
